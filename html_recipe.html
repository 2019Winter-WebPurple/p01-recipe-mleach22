Chocolate Chip Pancakes

These chocolate chip pancakes are fluffy, gooey, and delicious! They pair well with fruit, ice-cream, or whipped-cream! They are best served fresh and eaten with friends or family.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 3/4 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat your pan.
  2. Combine flour, sugar, cinnamon, baking powder and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Mix together wet ingredients and beat into dry mixture until smooth.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Pour or spoon batter into pan in desired quantity.
  6. Flip when top begins to bubble, then cook a minute more.
  7. Enjoy!

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Serves: 12

Serve warm with ice-cream, whipped-cream, or fruit and enjoy!
